1. The 3-Day Split (Alternating PPL Cycle)
Day 1: Push Day A

1 x Chest (Upper Chest)

1 x Chest (Middle Chest)

1 x Chest (Middle Chest / Fly or Pec Deck Machine)

1 x Shoulder (Front Delts)

1 x Shoulder (Side Delts)

1 x Triceps (Short Head)

1 x Triceps (Long Head)

Day 2: Pull Day A

2 x Back (Lats) (Note: Pulldown/Row variations used instead of Pull-ups)

1 x Back (Mid-Back & Rhomboids)

1 x Shoulder (Rear Delts)

1 x Biceps (Long Head)

1 x Biceps (Brachialis)

Day 3: Legs Day A

1 x Legs (Quads - Compound Free Weight e.g., Squat variant)

1 x Legs (Quads - Machine e.g., Extension)

1 x Legs (Hamstrings - Romanian Deadlift)

1 x Legs (Hamstrings - Seated Leg Curl)

1 x Legs (Adductors)

1 x Legs (Calves)

Day 4: Push Day B

1 x Chest (Upper Chest)

1 x Chest (Middle Chest)

1 x Chest (Lower Chest / Cable Fly variation)

1 x Shoulder (Front Delts)

1 x Shoulder (Side Delts)

1 x Triceps (Short Head)

1 x Triceps (Long Head)

Day 5: Pull Day B

1 x Back (Lats)

1 x Back (Mid-Back & Rhomboids)

1 x Back (Traps)

1 x Shoulder (Rear Delts)

2 x Biceps (Short Head)

Day 6: Legs Day B

1 x Legs (Quads - Compound Machine e.g., Leg Press variant)

1 x Legs (Quads - Machine e.g., Hack or Pendulum variant)

1 x Legs (Hamstrings - Lying Leg Curl)

1 x Legs (Hamstrings - Seated Leg Curl)

1 x Legs (Abductors)

1 x Legs (Calves)

2. The 4-Day Split (Consistency & Fixed Volume)
Day 1: Chest & Triceps

1 x Chest (Middle Chest)

1 x Chest (Upper Chest)

1 x Chest (Middle or Lower Chest / Cable Fly or Pec Deck Machine)

1 x Triceps (Short Head)

1 x Triceps (Long Head)

Day 2: Back

2 x Back (Lats) (Note: Pulldown/Row variations used instead of Pull-ups)

1 x Back (Mid-Back & Rhomboids)

1 x Back (Lower Back / Deadlift variant)

1 x Back (Traps)

1 x Shoulder (Rear Delts)

Day 3: Shoulder & Biceps

1 x Shoulder (Front Delts)
1 x Shoulder (Front Delts)

1 x Shoulder (Side Delts)

1 x Shoulder (Side Delts)

1 x Biceps (Long Head)

1 x Biceps (Short Head)

Day 4: Legs

1 x Legs (Quads - Free weight or Machine compound)

1 x Legs (Quads - Machine isolation)

1 x Legs (Hamstrings - RDL or Lying Curl)

1 x Legs (Hamstrings - Seated Leg Curl)

1 x Legs (Adductors or Abductors)

1 x Legs (Calves)

3. The 5-Day Split (Hypertrophy Bro-Split)
Day 1: Chest Day

2 x Chest (Middle Chest)

2 x Chest (Upper Chest)

1 x Chest (Lower Chest / Fly or Pec Deck variation)

Day 2: Back Day

2 x Back (Lats)

1 x Back (Mid-Back & Rhomboids)

1 x Back (Lower Back / Deadlift variant)

1 x Back (Traps)

Day 3: Arms Day

1 x Triceps (Long Head)

1 x Biceps (Long Head)

1 x Triceps (Lateral Head)

1 x Biceps (Short Head)

1 x Triceps (Short Head)

1 x Biceps (Brachioradialis / Forearms)

Day 4: Shoulder Day

2 x Shoulder (Front Delts)

2 x Shoulder (Side Delts)

1 x Shoulder (Rear Delts)

Day 5: Legs Day

2 x Legs (Quads - 1 Compound and 1 Isolation)

1 x Legs (Hamstrings - Seated Leg Curl)

1 x Legs (Hamstrings - Lying or RDL variation)

1 x Legs (Adductors or Abductors)

1 x Legs (Calves)
